2. Trading Cycle & Maintenance Rules

A cycle is a performance window. To be eligible for a reward, you must satisfy these Maintenance Rules during each specific time frame:

  • Active Trading Days: To qualify for a payout, you must be active on at least 5 separate days during your cycle.
  • The 3-Minute Quality Rule: A day is only recorded as "Active" if it contains at least one trade that was held open for 3 minutes or longer.

    • Note: It doesn't matter if you trade once or fifty times in a day; as long as at least one of those trades meets the 3-minute minimum, that entire day counts toward your 5-day requirement.

  • Risk Guardrails: You must strictly adhere to Daily Loss and Max Loss limits. A breach at any time fails the account.
  • The 40% Consistency Rule: No single trading day’s profit can exceed 40% of your total profit for that cycle.
  • Example: If your total profit is $1,000, your best day cannot exceed $400. If it does, you must continue trading to grow the total profit until that big day represents 40% or less.

What if you don't meet the rules?

  • In Loss: You cannot withdraw. Your cycle extends until you trade back into profit unless you breach the funded account by hitting your maximum loss limit.
  • Trading Days / Consistency Breach: You must continue trading until both the day-count and the profit-balance requirements are met.
  • 40% Consistency Rule Breach: You must continue trading to dilute the big win until it meets the 40% threshold.
