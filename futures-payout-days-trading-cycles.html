1. The Payout Schedule

Both account models operate on a 21-calendar-day trading cycle. The cycle begins automatically from the moment you place your first trade.

  • Fewpips Future Based CFDs - 1-Step: Performance Reward is available every 21 calendar days.
  • Fewpips Future Based CFDs - Instant Account: Performance Reward is available every 21 calendar days.

2. Trading Cycle & Maintenance Rules

A cycle is a performance window. To be eligible for a reward, you must satisfy all of the following Maintenance Rules during each cycle:


Active Trading Days

You must be active on at least 5 separate trading days during your cycle.


The 3-Minute Quality Rule

A day is only recorded as "Active" if it contains at least one trade that was held open for 3 minutes or longer.

Note: It doesn't matter if you trade once or fifty times in a day. As long as at least one of those trades meets the 3-minute minimum, that entire day counts toward your 5-day requirement.


Risk Guardrails

You must strictly adhere to Daily Loss and Max Loss limits at all times. A breach at any point immediately fails the account.

  • Fewpips Future Based CFDs - 1-Step: Max Daily Loss 3% of starting day balance. Max Total Loss 5% trailing drawdown.
  • Fewpips Future Based CFDs - Instant Account: Max Daily Loss 2% of starting day balance. Max Total Loss 4% trailing drawdown.

The Consistency Rule

No single trading day's profit can exceed the consistency threshold for your account type for that cycle.

  • Fewpips Future Based CFDs - 1-Step: No single day may exceed 40% of your total cycle profit.
  • Fewpips Future Based CFDs - Instant Account: No single day may exceed 25% of your total cycle profit.

Example (Future Based CFDs - 1-Step, $50,000 account, 5% target = $2,500):

Your best day cannot exceed 40% of total profit. If your total profit is $1,000, your best day cannot exceed $400. If it does, you must continue trading to grow total profit until that day represents 40% or less.


What if you don't meet the rules?

  • In Loss: You cannot withdraw. Your cycle extends until you trade back into profit, unless you breach the maximum loss limit.
  • Trading Days Breach: You must continue trading until the 5-day minimum is met.
  • Consistency Breach: You must continue trading to dilute the oversized day until it falls within the permitted threshold.

3. Payout Logistics


Timeline

Request → Audit (24–48 hrs) → Processing (24–48 hrs).

Total estimated lead time: 5–7 business days.


Minimum Withdrawal

$100 USD on all withdrawals.


Withdrawal Type

Full withdrawals are required. Partial withdrawals are not permitted. You may leave any amount you wish in the account as a base to continue trading.


Profit Split

80% to the trader / 20% to the company for both account models.


The Trading Day Rule

Once a withdrawal is processed, you must complete the required 5 active trading days in the new cycle (while maintaining all consistency and risk rules) to unlock your next withdrawal.


Account Refresh

You keep your same credentials. Once your withdrawal is confirmed, your account balance is updated and your new 21-day countdown begins with your next trade.

4. Full Withdrawal Policy

Full withdrawals are required on every cycle. No partial withdrawals are permitted. This policy exists to simplify administrative operations, reduce processing frequency, and maintain a clean record per account.


You may leave any amount you wish in the account as a base to continue trading into the next cycle.

5. Open Positions During Payout Request

Active trades are not allowed at the time of requesting a withdrawal. All positions must be fully closed before a withdrawal request can be submitted.
