Verification & KYC: Fast, Transparent, and Secure

At Fewpips, we believe that getting started shouldn't be a hurdle. Our Know Your Customer (KYC) process is designed to be as seamless as possible, ensuring a secure environment for all traders while getting you into the markets faster.

The Fundamentals

To get started, we require two mandatory pieces of information from all users:

  • Email Address (Mandatory)
  • Phone Number (Mandatory)

Payment-Specific Requirements

Depending on your chosen payment method, our compliance team may request additional documentation to verify your account.

1. Cryptocurrency Payments

If you deposit and withdraw using cryptocurrency, we offer a streamlined experience where no additional documents may be required. This is the fastest way to begin your trading journey.

2. Card & Digital Wallet Payments

For clients using traditional payment methods (Credit/Debit cards or Digital Wallets), we require standard verification documents to comply with global anti-fraud and banking regulations:

  • Proof of Identity: A high-quality copy of your Passport, Driving License, or National ID Card.
  • Proof of Address: A Utility Bill or Bank Statement issued within the last 3 months. (Alternatively, an Official ID showing your current address is also accepted).

The Fewpips Difference: Human Accuracy, Minute-Fast

While many firms rely on rigid AI bots that often reject documents for minor errors, Fewpips uses a Pro-Led Human Verification Process.

  • Lightning-Fast Turnaround: We aim to review and approve submissions within minutes.
  • Human Insight: Our specialists handle your data with care, ensuring that genuine documents are approved quickly without the frustrations of AI glitches.
  • Manual Precision: By choosing human experts over automated bots, we ensure a higher success rate and a more personalised experience for our traders.

Trader Tip: To ensure the fastest approval, please ensure all four corners of users documents are visible in the photo and that the text is clear and legible.

Why we do this:

Our KYC protocols are in place to prevent identity theft and ensure that every trader on the Fewpips platform is verified and legitimate. This protects our community and ensures the longevity of our funding partnerships.
